Executive Summary

This industrial training report documents the practical learning experience gained as an Information Technology student specializing in Networking during the tenure at Leviat Sdn Bhd, a company recognized for its expertise in network solutions and IT services. The training program was designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge acquired at the academic level and applications in the industry.

During the training period, exposure was provided to various aspects of network management, including network design, installation, configuration, troubleshooting, and security protocols. Hands-on tasks such as monitoring network performance and assisting in the maintenance of the company’s IT infrastructure were undertaken, which allowed a deeper understanding of networking concepts such as IP addressing, VLANs, firewall configurations, and server management in a professional environment to be gained. Technical skills were enhanced, and professional competencies, including problem-solving, teamwork, and effective communication, were developed. Best practices were observed and practical insights into IT support were obtained by working alongside experienced IT specialists.

In conclusion, a valuable platform was provided by Leviat Sdn Bhd for the application of academic theories in real-world settings, knowledge in networking technologies was enriched, and preparation for future professional challenges in the IT industry was achieved. This report serves as comprehensive documentation of the tasks undertaken, skills acquired, and lessons learned during the industrial training period.
