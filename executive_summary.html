Executive Summary

This industrial training report documents my practical learning experience as an Information Technology student specializing in Networking during my tenure at Leviat Sdn Bhd, a company recognized for its expertise in network solutions and IT services.

The training program was designed to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge acquired at the academic level and real-world industry applications.

Throughout the training period, I was exposed to various aspects of network management, including network design, installation, configuration, troubleshooting, and security protocols. I participated in hands-on tasks such as monitoring network performance and assisting in the maintenance of the company’s IT infrastructure.

This allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of networking concepts such as IP addressing, VLANs, firewall configurations, and server management in a professional environment.

The industrial training not only enhanced my technical skills but also developed my professional competencies, including problem-solving, teamwork, and effective communication. By working alongside experienced IT Specialists, I was able to observe best practices and gain practical insights into IT Support.

In conclusion, my industrial training at Leviat Sdn Bhd provided a valuable platform to apply academic theories in real-world settings, enriched my knowledge in networking technologies, and prepared me for future professional challenges in the IT industry.

This report serves as a comprehensive documentation of the tasks undertaken, skills acquired, and lessons learned during the industrial training period.
