Company Background

Leviat Sdn Bhd is a global leader in engineered solutions and innovative construction products, specialising in connecting, fixing and anchoring technologies for the construction and industrial sectors. The company’s trusted product brands are known for high performance, outstanding quality, and exceptional reliability. Its engineering expertise supports a wide range of applications, from structural connections and façade support systems to engineered solutions that enhance thermal and acoustic performance in buildings and infrastructure projects worldwide.

Mission

The mission of Leviat Sdn Bhd is to deliver advanced construction solutions that empower builders to create structures capable of standing the test of time, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, performance, and reliability in every product and service provided.

Services Provided

Leviat’s services and engineered solutions extend across multiple areas of construction support and structural technology. These include engineered systems for lifting, fixing, and anchoring components, technical design assistance throughout project stages, and bespoke solutions tailored to complex architecture or infrastructure needs. Its product and service portfolio also includes engineered fixing and connecting solutions for structural reinforcement, precast handling systems, and high‑performance construction accessories utilised in diverse sectors such as industrial, commercial, and residential projects.

The company leverages its extensive global engineering resources and manufacturing capability to provide responsive local services and technical support, assisting clients from initial planning through installation and project completion.
