Company Background

Leviat Sdn Bhd is a global leader in engineered solutions and innovative construction products, specialising in connecting, fixing and anchoring technologies for the construction and industrial sectors. The company’s trusted product brands are known for high performance, outstanding quality, and exceptional reliability. Its engineering expertise supports a wide range of applications, from structural connections and façade support systems to engineered solutions that enhance thermal and acoustic performance in buildings and infrastructure projects worldwide.

Mission

The mission of Leviat Sdn Bhd is to deliver advanced construction solutions that empower builders to create structures capable of standing the test of time, while maintaining the highest standards of quality, performance, and reliability in every product and service provided.

Services Provided

- Engineered systems for lifting, fixing, and anchoring construction components.

- Technical design support during different stages of a project.

- Custom engineering solutions for complex buildings or infrastructure.

- Fixing and connecting products for structural reinforcement.

- Precast handling systems for construction work.

- Construction accessories used in industrial, commercial, and residential projects.

- Engineering expertise and manufacturing support from global resources.

- Technical assistance from project planning until completion.
