Introduction of INDUSTRIAL TRAINING

Industrial training is an important part of higher education, especially for students in technical and professional fields. The knowledge learned in the classroom is applied in a real working environment through industrial training. Practical experience is gained, technical skills are improved, and a better understanding of how organizations operate in the industry is developed.

This industrial training was carried out at Leviat Sdn Bhd. During the training period, placement was given in the Information Technology (IT) department. Various IT-related tasks were performed such as installing software, troubleshooting computer issues, configuring printers, assisting with basic network tasks, and providing technical support to users.


Objective of INDUSTRIAL TRAINING

1. To apply knowledge learned in class

This training allows students to use the knowledge learned during their studies in a real working environment.

2. To gain practical experience

Students can improve their skills by performing tasks such as installing software, troubleshooting computers, and assisting users.

3. To understand the working environment

Industrial training helps students learn about teamwork, communication, and responsibilities in the workplace.



Importance of INDUSTRIAL TRAINING

Skills enhancement.

Industry relevance.

Increased job opportunities.
