Introduction of INDUSTRIAL TRAINING

Industrial training is an important part of higher education, especially for students in technical and professional fields. It gives students the opportunity to apply the knowledge learned in the classroom to real working environments. Through industrial training, students can gain practical experience, improve their technical skills, and understand how organizations operate in the industry.

This industrial training was carried out at Leviat Sdn Bhd. During the training period, I was assigned to the Information Technology (IT) department. I was involved in several IT-related tasks such as installing software, troubleshooting computer issues, configuring printers, assisting with basic network tasks, and providing technical support to users.

Objective of INDUSTRIAL TRAINING

1. To apply knowledge learned in class

This training allows students to use the knowledge learned during their studies in a real working environment.

2. To gain practical experience

Students can improve their skills by performing tasks such as installing software, troubleshooting computers, and assisting users.

3. To understand the working environment

Industrial training helps students learn about teamwork, communication, and responsibilities in the workplace.
